About Us

Neogarden Systems was born from a simple idea: that the future of food is personal, intelligent, and beautiful. We design climate-controlled grow sheds that integrate seamlessly into smart homes and upscale properties — turning underused outdoor space into powerful, year-round food systems.

Engineered with automation and crafted with intention, Neogarden marries cutting-edge hydroponics with minimalist architecture. The result? Clean food. Elevated living. And a quiet revolution in your backyard.

Who We Are

Our team brings together over 15 years of combined experience in sustainable architecture, smart agriculture, and automated growing systems. We’re designers, engineers, and growers who believe food security and elegant design should go hand in hand.

Our Mission

To make self-sufficient food production effortless and beautiful — for families, innovators, and communities who want more control over what they grow and what they eat.

Whether you’re an urban professional, a tech-forward homesteader, or a wellness-minded investor, Neogarden was built for you.
